Privacy Policy — Weather 2050

Last updated: March 2026

Weather 2050 is a personal, non-commercial app built for learning purposes. It is free to use and contains no advertising.

What data the app accesses

Location
The app requests your device's GPS location once per session (and on manual refresh). Your coordinates are used solely to:

  • fetch a 7-day weather forecast from the Open-Meteo API (open-meteo.com), and
  • look up the nearest grid cell in the bundled CMIP6/CORDEX climate delta dataset.

Your location is never stored on any server, never shared with third parties beyond the API call described above, and never used for advertising or tracking.

What data is stored on your device

The app caches ERA5 historical climate data (temperature and precipitation statistics for your approximate location, rounded to the nearest 0.5° grid cell) in the device's local storage. This avoids repeated network calls. The cache contains no personally identifiable information and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.
